Financing FAQs

Common questions about our financing options

What projects qualify for financing?

We offer financing for inground pools, above ground pools, and deck construction projects.

How long does approval take?

Both lenders offer quick approval processes. You can often receive a credit decision within the same day of applying.

What are the typical interest rates?

Interest rates vary based on credit score, loan amount, and term length. Both lenders offer competitive rates. Apply to see your personalized rate.

Do I need perfect credit to qualify?

No, both lenders work with a range of credit profiles. Apply to see what options are available for your situation.

Are there any prepayment penalties?

HFS Financial offers loans with no prepayment penalties. Check with Lyon Financial for their specific terms.

Can I apply for both lenders?

Yes, you can apply to both lenders to compare rates and terms, then choose the option that works best for you.
